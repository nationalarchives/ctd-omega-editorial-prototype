COAL 80/80/11 Content: Folio 36. Letter from Samuel Harding, Vestry Clerk, Newcastle under Lyme, to the Poor Law Commission. He had been requested by the churchwardens who wished to know what measures they needed to take with regard to the Registration Act as they had received no communication from the assistant poor law commissioners about it. The act seemed to give them the power to start temporary registers and appoint a temporary registrar, and he was applying for that post. He added a note that he would be applying for the post of clerk to the board of guardians when it became available. Paper Number: 5211/C/1837. Poor Law Union Number 421. Counties: Staffordshire. 1960
